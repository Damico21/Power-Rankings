Round 8 Wrap
Round 8 · The Table Gets Interesting.
Round 8 · 2026 Season
Holmes Stay Up.
But FlagDons Are Climbing.
Round 8 arrived and delivered what this competition always does — some surprises, some confirmations, and a table that looks slightly different to the one we started with. Holmes and Gardens won again, as they seem to do. Broncic put up a big number. Van Twists beat Cripps Nipps convincingly. But the story of the round — the thing that'll be talked about at the top of the table — is FlagDons.
Rick's mob dropped 1,170 on Zak's IZaks. That's not a fluke. That's a statement. FlagDons jump three spots in the PPR table, from 8th to 5th, and they're not done climbing. Meanwhile, DC & Tax Payers had a rough one — beaten by the Dark Magicians — and fall four spots to 6th. The table above 8th is starting to become genuinely contested.
Bottom line: eight rounds in and the top is holding, but the middle is on fire. Strap in — it's getting good.
