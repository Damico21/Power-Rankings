Four rounds in and the Ligma League is starting to separate the contenders from the pretenders. The early season excuses are gone — no more "still finding my feet," no more "settling in." By now you are what your record says you are. This week's card has a statement win, a rough night for someone who really needed a result, and a Lock of the Week so obvious I'm almost embarrassed to write it. Almost.
Broncic ~1,150 def. IZaks ~960 +190
Broncic scraps their way to another one. William's mob have made an art form of winning the games they're supposed to. The IZaks came in sitting 10th for a reason, and Broncic had far too much hunger to let this one slip. Nothing flashy — just two points in the bag and a quiet nod to the table. Quietly building. Quietly dangerous.
Phillipou Cumming ~940 def. Cripps Nipps ~930 +10
Phillipou Cumming are not going away. Cam's team are accumulating wins like frequent flyer points right now and another one gets banked here — though don't expect it to be pretty. Cripps Nipps will make them work for every single point. Flashes of quality, but never quite enough to get over the line when it matters most. Story of their season so far.
💥 Landslide
Dark Magicians ~870 def. Nethers Men ~715 +155
Dark Magicians win a comfortable one and keep the pressure on. Osei's side have too much firepower for a Nethers Men outfit that has been underwhelming through the opening rounds. Max's crew will be searching for answers after this one — a gap that wide is hard to explain away. Back to the drawing board for Nethers.
FlagDons ~1,095 def. DC & Tax Payers ~985 +110
Rick's FlagDons put on a show. A commanding win over DC & Tax Payers is the kind of performance that earns serious respect around the competition. FlagDons are locked in, firing, and absolutely must-watch right now. DC drop a spot but remain firmly in the top three conversation.
🔒 Lock of the Week
Shava Girthi ~1,200 def. Van Twists ~1,075 +125
Patrick's Shava Girthis deliver when it counts most. This is the Lock of the Week and it's not going to be close. Van Twists are sitting deep for a reason, and Shava Girthi have far too much class to let this one slip away. This competition has been crying out for a banker — here it is. Lock incoming. Bank it.
⭐ Match of the Round
Holmes & Gardens ~1,160 def. Foopys ~1,080 +80
Holmes and Gardens keep rolling. Matthew's mob are putting up numbers that are genuinely frightening right now. Four rounds at the top and no signs of slowing down — this is the match everyone's watching, and Holmes and Gardens are the benchmark this competition is being measured against. Foopys will push, they always do. But that top spot isn't going anywhere. Everyone else is just chasing.
