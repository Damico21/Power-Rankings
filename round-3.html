💸 Will's Weekly Wagers

Round 3 Edition
Round 3 and the gloves are officially off. The honeymoon period is well and truly over — every team has had enough time to read the room, patch the holes, and figure out who they really are. This week's card is stacked. We've got a comeback, a throttling, and a bloke at the top of the rankings who apparently can't stop winning. Let's do it.
🔒 Lock of the Week
Holmes and Gardens def. Flagdons
1,192 – 1,041  (+151)
Holmes and Gardens march straight to the top and they're not apologising for it. A 151-point dismantling of the new number-twos is the kind of statement that gets the league buzzing. Matthew's mob look completely unplayable right now — deep, consistent, and absolutely ruthless when it counts. Flagdons tried. They just weren't ready for this.
📋 Result
Phillipou def. DC & Tax Payers
1,088 – 997  (+91)
The upset of the round. DC looked locked in all week and then completely unravelled. Meanwhile Phillipou — quiet for a few rounds — showed up with something to prove and delivered in spades. A 91-point win that rockets them into the top five and sends a shiver down the spine of the top end of town.
💥 Landslide
Izaks def. Van Twists
1,063 – 873  (+190)
Van Twists in freefall. Three rounds in and Tim's side have tumbled all the way to 12th. The Izaks, on the other hand, are turning into a genuine mid-table force. Zak's team dropped 190 on them without breaking a sweat — 190! The Van Twist rollercoaster officially has more downs than ups this season.
📋 Result
Broncic def. Foopys
1,047 – 1,029  (+18)
Broncic digs deep and edges it by 18. Miller's Foopys were right there — that 350-point demolition from last week feels like ancient history now. William's Broncic held firm when it mattered and walked away with two points that look enormous in hindsight. Foopys slide from 2nd to 4th and have questions to answer.
📋 Result
Dark Magicians def. Nethers men
1,031 – 944  (+87)
Dark Magicians doing just enough. They're not blowing teams away like Round 1, but Osei's side keep finding a way to get the job done. An 87-point win over Nethers Men is comfortable without being convincing — but in this competition, you take the two points and ask no further questions.
⭐ Match of the Round
Shavas def. Cripps Nipps
986 – 971  (+15)
Fifteen points. That's it. Fifteen. Patrick's Shavas nearly gave it away in the final quarter before grinding out a result that had everyone in the group chat going absolutely feral. Cripps Nipps — written off by half the competition — pushed hard and nearly pulled off the heist of the season. This one had more twists than the Van Twists. In a better week.

💥 Takeaways

  • Holmes and Gardens are the real deal — 1st place and they don't look like giving it up.
  • Phillipou absolutely cooked DC & Tax Payers. Top five now and surging.
  • Van Twists crashing hard — 12th after starting as one of the fancied sides. Ugly.
  • Broncic keep finding a way. Classic over-achievers — the competition's quiet assassin.
  • Cripps Nipps showing signs of life. Don't sleep on them in Round 4.
  • Izaks are building something real. 190-point wins don't lie.
Three rounds deep and it's already a mess at the top. Wouldn't have it any other way.
