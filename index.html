What is the
Ligma League?

The Ligma League is a 12-team AFL Fantasy competition where bragging rights are everything and no lead is ever safe. Founded in 2024, the league has quickly become a hotbed of trade controversy, trash talk, and the occasional miracle comeback.

Every week, Paz drops the official Power Rankings — an unbiased, ruthlessly honest assessment of where every team stands. If you're at the bottom, you earned it.
