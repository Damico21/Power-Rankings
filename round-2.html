Round 2 Edition

Welcome back, punters. Round 2 is here and the early pecking order is already getting shaken up. After a barnstorming opening round, the field has a clearer picture of who's legit — and who got lucky. This week we've got a landslide, a squeaker, and a match of the round for the ages. Let's get into it.
Dark Magicians def. Phillipou +132
1,057 – 925

No surprises here. The Dark Magicians are operating at a different level right now — clinical, dominant, and utterly ruthless. Phillipou showed up but there was simply no stopping Osei's mob. A 132-point thumping that sends a crystal-clear message to the rest of the competition.

VANTWISTFC def. 30/09/2023ers +57
1,144 – 1,087

The Van Twists bounce back. After a week where they couldn't get anything going, Tim's outfit reminded everyone exactly why they're top-tier. A 57-point win over Rupe's crew is no fluke — the Van Twists look hungry and they're not done making a statement.

foopy is lyfe def. Cripps Nipps +350
1,023 – 673

Absolute annihilation. Miller's Foopys put on a masterclass and Cripps Nipps simply had no answer. A 350-point margin is the kind of scoreline that haunts you in your sleep. Hayden will be hoping the selectors don't look too hard at the tape this week.

IZaks def. Nethers Men +106
1,020 – 914

The IZaks notch their first win of the year and it's a comfortable one. Zak's side found their rhythm against Max's outfit, putting together a disciplined performance top to bottom. A confidence-boosting result that could be the catalyst for a run up the table.

FlagDons2026 def. Broncic +20
1,064 – 938

The FlagDons edge it by 20 in a contest that was tighter than it looked. Rick's side ground it out, though William's Broncic made them work for every point. A scrappy, hard-nosed result — exactly the kind of game that defines a season.

Holmes and Gardens def. Shava Girthi +8
1,158 – 1,150

Eight points. EIGHT. Matthew's Holmes and Gardens cling on in the most nerve-shredding finish of the round. Patrick pushed the top-ranked side to the very brink and will rue the one that got away. This one had everything — momentum swings, late drama, and a result that could have gone either way. Footy.

💥 Takeaways

  • Dark Magicians are the real deal — 132-point wins don't happen by accident.
  • Van Twists back to their best. The Round 1 blip already looks like a distant memory.
  • Foopys with a 350-point demolition — the most terrifying scoreline of the season so far.
  • IZaks finally on the board. Watch them build from here.
  • Holmes and Gardens surviving by 8 says as much about Shava Girthi's improvement as their own quality.
  • Cripps Nipps and Nethers Men need to answer some serious questions this week.

Another cracking round in the books. Keep the trades sharp and the excuses sharper.
